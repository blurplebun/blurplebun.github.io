Info

Nadir can be seen as the inverse of Hizen. While often portrayed as hell, it isn’t fiery or chaotic. Instead, it’s quiet, dark, and dreadfully still, as though watched by an omnipresent gaze. Anything that enters this void slowly desaturates and decays as its essence of life fades away.
