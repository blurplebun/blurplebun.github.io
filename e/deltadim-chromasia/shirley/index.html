Shirley is an orange cat-fox hybrid with white fur on her ears, muzzle, chest, abdomen, and the tip of her tail. Her eyes are yellow and her pawpads and inner ears are orange.

She lives with her boyfriend Rose, and works as a barista at a local cafe in Chromasia. She also loves gardening and taking care of pretty flowers.
