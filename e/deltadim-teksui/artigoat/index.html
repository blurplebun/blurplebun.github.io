Artigoat

Species: Goat
Pronouns: He
Gender: Male
Sexuality: Gay
Nickname: Arti, Baba, Goat
Artigoat is a white male goat with yellow fur pattern on his legs and ears, as well as having a pair of short pointy horns. He also has a pair of hooves as his feet.

Arti has a hobby of reading books, writing stories, and play saxophone. He lives in an apartment with his partner Artipup and works as a cashier at a local supermarket in Teksui.
